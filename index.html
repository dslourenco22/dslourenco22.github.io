Our Amazing Board

We are a community of passionate individuals dedicated to the advancement and celebration of excellence in computer science. Founded on the principles of academic achievement, leadership, and innovation, our society aims to foster a supportive environment for students who are driven to excel in the ever-evolving field of computing. Through a combination of educational events, networking opportunities, and collaborative projects, we strive to empower our members to reach their full potential and make meaningful contributions to the world of technology.

Induction Ceremony

The CSHS Induction Ceremony is a special event held to recognize and celebrate the achievements of students who have demonstrated excellence in computer science and related fields. During this event, new members are formally inducted into the society, often through a ceremony that includes speeches, presentations, and the awarding of certificates or pins.

Hour of Code

Hour of Code is a global initiative aimed at introducing people of all ages to the basics of computer programming and coding through engaging and accessible tutorials and activities. During Hour of Code events, participants are encouraged to spend at least one hour exploring coding concepts and completing interactive challenges, regardless of their prior experience or background in technology.

  • Date: December 7-8 2023

Family Code Night

Family Code Night is an initiative aimed at introducing children and their families to computer programming and coding concepts in a fun and interactive way. It typically involves hosting events or workshops where families can come together to learn the basics of coding through activities, games, and projects. These events often emphasize collaboration, problem-solving, and creativity, allowing family members to bond while also gaining valuable skills in technology and programming. Family Code Night promotes digital literacy and encourages families to explore the exciting world of coding together.

  • Date: February 15 2024

Girls Who Code

The girls in the Computer Science Honor Society held an event called “Girls Who Code” on March 5th 2024. It was an incredible success, and all the girls who attended and the hosts had an amazing time. When our participants arrived we began with a guest speaker explaining her experiences in the field, and how she uses her computer science knowledge in a male dominated environment. We then continued to teach them about the basics of Python and Jeroo to provide a simple understanding. Then they proceeded to experiment coding on their own. To end the day, we asked some trivia questions based on what they learned and provided everyone with prizes. It was a very successful and enjoyable experience, and our society appreciated everyone who came!

  • Date: March 5th 2024
